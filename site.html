How It Works

Our analytical engine combines structured intelligence tradecraft with computational modeling.

01

Probability Engine

Calibrated likelihood ratios. Accuracy-scored. Every probability is defensible and auditable, with full update history.

02

Open-Source Intelligence Fusion

// about

EchoFrame

EchoFrame builds operational intelligence platforms for energy companies and commodity traders who need a live, quantified view of political risk. We don't publish reports. We build always-on dashboards that fuse hundreds of open-source signals into calibrated probability estimates, updated in real time.

Our platforms cover the Middle East, South America, the EU energy landscape, and Mexico's energy sector. Each one tracks specific actors, maps power networks, and runs predictive scenario models that have demonstrated from 30-day up to 90-day average lead times ahead of major geopolitical shifts.

Clients include energy majors navigating regulatory exposure, commodity trading desks pricing sovereign risk, and hedge funds with energy and resource positions. EchoFrame's models have been credited with saving clients millions in compliance costs and generating over $100M in trading value.

EchoFrame is the intelligence platform arm of The OSINT Collective, a consortium of analysts, technologists, and regional specialists providing sovereign intelligence capabilities worldwide.

The Collective was founded on a simple premise: the best geopolitical intelligence comes from fusing open-source data at scale with deep regional expertise and rigorous analytical tradecraft. Our team combines backgrounds in investigative journalism, risk advisory, defense intelligence, and AI engineering to deliver actionable foresight, not after-the-fact analysis.
