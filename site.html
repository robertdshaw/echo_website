How It Works

Our analytical engine combines structured intelligence tradecraft with computational modeling.

01

Bayesian Engine

Calibrated likelihood ratios. Brier-scored. Max ±8pp per update. Every probability is defensible and auditable.

02

OSINT Fusion

ADS-B, AIS, NASA FIRMS, GDELT, 100+ regional news sources. Cross-referenced and tiered by reliability.

03

Source Tiering

3-tier confidence weighting. Full provenance tracking. Every claim traced to origin and scored for reliability.

04

Scenario Modeling

Monte Carlo simulations. Divergent futures with confidence bands. Game-theory modeling for multi-actor scenarios.

The OSINT Collective

EchoFrame is the intelligence platform arm of The OSINT Collective — a consortium of analysts, technologists, and regional specialists providing sovereign intelligence capabilities to government and enterprise clients.
